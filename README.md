# ECONOGRAPHY: BTC Memory Engine

**ECONOGRAPHY** is a creative-technology research prototype that transforms Bitcoin market data into a living visual memory system.

The project explores the idea that financial data is not only numerical information, but also a trace of collective behavior, pressure, liquidity, volatility, risk and market emotion. Instead of presenting Bitcoin only through conventional charts, ECONOGRAPHY maps market signals into a visual organism and curated generative artworks.

## Core Idea

Money remembers.

Markets leave traces of fear, confidence, compression, expansion, liquidity and instability. ECONOGRAPHY interprets these traces as visual memory.

The system translates Bitcoin market signals into visual behaviors such as:

- orbit movement
- particle density
- liquidity flow
- volatility distortion
- risk pressure
- core pulse
- vascular data connections
- generative market-state artworks

## Website Structure

The website is organized into three main layers:

### 1. BTC Memory Organism

An interactive visual organism generated from Bitcoin market data.

It maps selected market signals into a living structure where:

- price shapes the memory orbit
- volume increases particle density
- volatility creates distortion
- liquidity controls flow continuity
- risk creates pressure, scars and compression
- market state affects the core and visual atmosphere

### 2. Art Space

A curated visual gallery of selected BTC market states.

Each artwork represents a different market condition, such as:

- current compressed liquidity
- stress and drawdown
- record expansion
- fracture and instability

These artworks are artistic interpretations of market behavior, not trading signals or financial predictions.

### 3. Research

A research explanation page that presents:

- the purpose of the project
- the data-to-visual mapping logic
- what is live and what is interpreted
- the current limitations of the prototype
- the roadmap for future development

## Data Transparency

This project is a working prototype.

Some parts are data-driven, while others are interpreted demo states.

### Live Range Mode

When available, the BTC page uses normalized Bitcoin historical data for selected time ranges such as 1D, 7D and 30D.

### Saved Date Markers

Dates such as Apr 7, Jul 10 and Feb 5 are currently used as interpreted historical markers. They represent selected market states until exact historical date fetching is connected.

### Art Space Images

The Art Space artworks are curated generative interpretations of market states. They are not investment advice, trading signals or predictions.

## Visual Mapping Logic

| Market Signal | Visual Meaning |
|---|---|
| Price | Orbit anchor and market position |
| Volume | Particle density and golden data texture |
| Volatility | Distortion, turbulence and unstable movement |
| Liquidity | Cyan flow and smooth continuity |
| Risk | Magenta/red pressure, scars and compression |
| Market State | Overall mood of the organism |

## Current Limitations

This version is not the final research system.

The current prototype still needs:

- exact historical date fetching
- verified time-series windows for saved markers
- richer market datasets
- reproducible data pipeline documentation
- exportable artwork snapshots
- stronger research references and methodology documentation

## Roadmap

Future improvements may include:

- order book depth integration
- liquidation flow data
- ETF flow or macro indicator layers
- real-time artwork generation from market states
- downloadable generative snapshots
- stronger visual analytics layer
- public research documentation

## Disclaimer

ECONOGRAPHY is a visual research and creative-technology prototype.

It is not financial advice.  
It is not a trading system.  
It is not a prediction engine.

The project is designed to explore how financial data can become visual memory, generative art and an interpretable creative interface.
